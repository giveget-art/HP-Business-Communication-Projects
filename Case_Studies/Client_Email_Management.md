# Client Email Management

## Before
Emails were informal and lacked clear call-to-action.

Example:
"Hi, just checking if you saw the proposal."

## After (Improved Version)

Subject: Follow-Up on Proposal Submission – Response Request

Dear [Client Name],

I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to follow up on the proposal submitted on [Date]. Kindly confirm if any additional clarification is required.

Looking forward to your response.

Best regards,
[Your Name]

## Result
- More professional tone
- Clear action request
- Faster client responses
