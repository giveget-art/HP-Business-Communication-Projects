
# Team Efficiency Case Study

## Problem
The team frequently missed deadlines due to unclear email instructions and unstructured meetings.

## Action Taken
- Introduced structured email format:
  - Clear subject lines
  - Bullet-point instructions
  - Defined deadlines
- Implemented meeting agenda template
- Assigned action owners for every task

## Result
- Deadline adherence improved by 50%
- Reduced back-and-forth clarification emails
- Improved accountability within team

## Key Learning
Clarity saves time. Structure reduces chaos.
